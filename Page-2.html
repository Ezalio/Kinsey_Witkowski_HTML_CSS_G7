A recap of Bella Hadid's biggest year yet

Her career went stratospheric a while ago, but 2022 has really been a landmark year for Bella Hadid. The 26-year-old was a constant presence, from the Fall-Winter 2022 and Spring-Summer 2023 runways to campaign billboards and magazine covers.

Bella Hadid was voted model of the year 2022

To top it all off, at the Fashion Awards, presented by Diet Coke, Bella was announced as the winner in the Model of The Year category, having been nominated alongside Adut Akech, Lila Moss, Paloma Elsesser, and Quannah Chasinghorse. As the model gets set to enjoy an even merrier Christmas, Vogue revisits some highlights from Bella's big year.

January

Bella kicked off 2022 with Versace's Spring-Summer 2022 campaign, for which she was photographed with her supermodel sister, Gigi Hadid. She also starred in a nostalgic campaign for the rebooted Noughties line Miss Sixty, photographed by Zoey Grossman and styled by Haley Woollens.

February

The super walked in 10 shows in February alone as fashion month kicked off, including the poignant Off-White show that kicked off Paris Fashion Week and featured the couture-like pieces founder Virgil Abloh had been working on before his untimely death from cancer in November 2021.

March

As fashion month rumbled on, Bella walked for Burberry, Givenchy, Ludovic de Saint Sernin, Ralph Lauren, Coperni, Isabel Marant, Sacai, and Andreas Kronthaler for Vivienne Westwood.

April

US Vogue unveiled Bella as its April 2022 cover star. Ethan James Green captured a candid photo of her wearing Maison Margiela Artisanal, designed by John Galliano, and styled by fashion editor Gabriella Karefa-Johnson. In an interview with Rob Haskell, she opened up about her life off the runway – including her battles with depression, anxiety and Lyme disease.

May

Bella draped herself in jewels for Swarovski's Spring-Summer 2022 campaign, which showed her in colorful crystal necklaces, earrings, bracelets and rings.

June

Mugler's creative director Casey Cadwallader called upon Bella, Chloë Sevigny, Dominique Jackson, Megan Thee Stallion and Amber Valletta (to name a few) to feature in his high-octane Spring-Summer 2022 video, directed by Torso.

July

Bella modeled for two major campaigns in July: Fendi's Fall-Winter 2022 ad, shot by Craig McDean, and Marc Jacobs's denim monogram campaign, photographed by Mario Sorrenti.

August

She modeled in an otherworldly editorial shot by Elizaveta Porodina, as seen in British Vogue's August 2022 issue.

September

Another fashion month, and Bella walked for the usual big names including Fendi, Tom Ford and Versace, as well as appearing in Victoria Beckham’s first show in Paris and the inaugural Vogue World event.

October

Lest we forget Bella's viral moment at Coperni. Videos of the super being spray-painted live at the show set social media ablaze, contributing to one of the most memorable moments of the Spring-Summer 2023 season.

November

Bella was cast alongside Emily Ratajkowski, Adwoa Aboah, Ashley Graham and Precious Lee in the 2023 Pirelli calendar, photographed by Emma Summerton. Bella played “The Sprite” in the project, which was titled Love Letters To The Muse.

December

The super rounded off her successful year with a win at the Fashion Awards. She was named Model of the Year at the ceremony at the Royal Albert Hall, one of the biggest nights of the year on Britain’s industry calendar. Bella didn’t make it to collect her award in person, so we missed out on a red-carpet moment – but the stars who did attend pulled out all the stops.
